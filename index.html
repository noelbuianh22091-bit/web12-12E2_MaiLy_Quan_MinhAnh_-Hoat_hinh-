THE AMAZING WORLD OF GUMBALL

Adventures in Elmore

The Amazing World of Gumball is a British-American animated series created by Ben Bocquelet for Cartoon Network. It follows the misadventures of 12-year-old cat Gumball Watterson, his adoptive goldfish brother Darwin, and their family in the fictional city of Elmore. Accompanied by bizarre friends and family, Gumball's schemes often lead to hilarious chaos. The show blends 2D, 3D, and live-action for a unique style, running from 2011 to 2019 with 240 episodes.
